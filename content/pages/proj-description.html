Project Description

Black-Eyed Susies explores the culture of rock climbing from the perspective of a woman. Climbing is a male dominated area, as are most sports. It can be intimidating for young girls and women to partake in something if it seems like a boys club. I conducted interviews with female climbers to learn about their personal experiences participating in this activity and to see what was missing from the community. I wanted to find women of various backgrounds to see how that affects their understanding of climbing. From this information I gained an empathetic understanding of the users needs. I designed and developed an application that could help bring the community together and inspire others to join. I also gathered content on the history of climbing, and notable women that pioneered the way for modern women in mountaineering.
